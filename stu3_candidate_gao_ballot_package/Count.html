If system is present, it SHALL be UCUM. If present, the value SHALL a whole number.</p><p>XPath:</p><blockquote><pre>(f:code or not(f:value)) and (not(exists(f:system)) or (f:system/@value='http://unitsofmeasure.org&apos; and f:code/@value='1')) and not(contains(f:value/@value, '.'))</pre></blockquote></div>
